Map Britannia

A narrative-led map of cultural, literary, heritage and dark tourism locations across the UK. Discover the stories behind the places — from plague outbreaks to shipwrecks, battlefields to literary landscapes.

Built as an independent project. Currently in prototype stage, expanding nationally.

Categories

  • Literary — authors, novels and places associated with British literature
  • Military — battlefields, airfields, fortifications and military heritage
  • Horrible History — executions, plague, crime and dark tourism locations
  • Maritime — shipwrecks and coastal heritage
  • Ancient Landscape — ancient and veteran trees, lost settlements and prehistoric sites
